Field Manual: Key Terminology

Know the language. Survive the regime.

🏠 The Regime

Your household. Governed by a tiny, irrational leader who controls when you eat, sleep, and use the bathroom.

👶 Hostile Takeover

When a new baby arrives and the existing dictator must now share power. Rarely goes smoothly.

🛏️ Coup Attempt

Any bedtime resistance lasting more than 45 minutes. Success rate for parents: approximately 0%.

👴 Diplomatic Immunity

The special status grandparents enjoy that allows them to break every rule you've established.

💩 War Crimes

Diaper blowouts. Specifically the ones that happen in public, in the car, or right after you've changed them.

📋 Intelligence Briefing

What other parents call "parenting advice." We call it survival intel from the field.
